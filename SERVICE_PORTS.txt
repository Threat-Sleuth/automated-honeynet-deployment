------------

HOST_IP
-------
The installer determines the first host IPv4 address and stores it in:

/etc/default/honeynet

Portainer is pinned to portainer/portainer-ce:2.39.5 and started with
--no-setup-token.

WARNING
-------
These credentials and vulnerable services are intended only for an isolated,
